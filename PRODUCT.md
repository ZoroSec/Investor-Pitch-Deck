# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Startup founders (pre-seed to Series A) preparing to raise capital. They land here from social/referral links, evaluating in seconds whether this tool is credible enough to trust with their pitch. Secondary: VCs and accelerator staff checking what their founders are using.

## Product Purpose

"Pitch your investor" is an AI fundraising coach: it interviews founders, builds their narrative and deck, simulates tough investor Q&A, and scores fundability. The landing page's job is to convert a skeptical, ambitious founder into a free-tier signup. Success = the page itself feels like proof the product has taste and rigor.

## Brand Personality

Ambitious, polished, premium. The Linear/Stripe/Vercel register: dark, precise, engineered. Copy is direct and confident — founder-to-founder, no fluff, no hype adjectives. The page should feel like software VCs themselves would respect.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-SaaS slop: uniform icon-card grids, eyebrow labels above every section, gradient text everywhere, glassmorphism on every surface, big-number/small-label metric rows.
- Warm-light minimalist editorial (explicitly rejected by the founder — dark premium won).
- Crypto-landing excess: rainbow glows, 3D blobs, animated everything.

## Design Principles

1. **Practice what you preach** — a pitch-coaching product must itself pitch flawlessly; every section is an argument, not a template block.
2. **Restraint spends better** — one glow, one gradient, one glass surface where it counts beats ten by reflex.
3. **Show the product, not adjectives** — mockups, simulated investor dialogue, and scores do the persuading.
4. **Typography carries the premium** — committed scale contrast and tight hierarchy, not decoration.
5. **Motion with intent** — entrances choreographed per section's content; nothing animates just because it can.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1 on dark backgrounds, large text ≥3:1. `prefers-reduced-motion` honored globally (already wired in globals.css). Keyboard-reachable interactive elements (tabs, accordion, nav).
